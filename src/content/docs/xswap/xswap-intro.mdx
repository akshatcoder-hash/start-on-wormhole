### 🚴‍♀️ The Hub-and-Spoke Model 🚴‍♂️

Picture a bicycle wheel for a moment. The hub is the wheel's core, and the spokes are the rods connecting the hub to the tire. In our DeFi app, the hub is the command center where all the action happens, and the spokes are the various blockchains we'll connect to. This setup makes our app super efficient and user-friendly. 🌟
