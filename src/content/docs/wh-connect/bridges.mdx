Wormhole, now a renowned cross-chain interoperability protocol, had humble beginnings as a basic token bridge between Ethereum and Solana in its first version. 🌉 It wasn't until August 2021 that Wormhole v2 was unveiled as the full-fledged protocol we know today. 🚀

Ready to roll up your sleeves and dive in? 🛠️ Let's get hands-on and build our very own token bridge using [Wormhole Connect](https://wormhole.com/connect/).
